## ðŸš€ 3-Step Quick Deploy

### Step 1: Create Signing Key (5 min)
```powershell
cd C:\Users\TechShack\Desktop\TCEMS\android

keytool -genkey -v -keystore tcems-release-key.jks `
  -keyalg RSA -keysize 2048 -validity 10000 `
  -alias tcems-key-alias
```

### Step 2: Build Release (3 min)
```powershell
./gradlew bundleRelease `
  -Pandroid.injected.signing.store.file=tcems-release-key.jks `
  -Pandroid.injected.signing.store.password=YOUR_PASSWORD `
  -Pandroid.injected.signing.key.alias=tcems-key-alias `
  -Pandroid.injected.signing.key.password=YOUR_PASSWORD
```

### Step 3: Upload to Play Store (10 min)
1. Go to https://play.google.com/console
2. Create app listing (if not done)
3. Upload: `app/build/outputs/bundle/release/app-release.aab`
4. Publish

**Total Time: ~70 minutes** (including setup)
